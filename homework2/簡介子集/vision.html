Profile(Vision)

Development focus

  • Cultivate professionals in international transportation and telecommunications.
  • Encourage the publication of international journals and improve the level of academic research.
  • Integrate academic and practice, improve research capacity, and assist enterprises to improve operating efficiency.

Development Strategy

  • Curriculum: Promote the study of the four major professional fields to achieve a complete course study.
  • Admissions: Provide scholarships for freshmen to actively recruit outstanding students.
  • Research: Promote the establishment of field funds, encourage participation in national research competitions, and publish research results in international seminars.
  • Service: Strengthen cooperation with enterprises in various fields, and improve the operating performance of enterprises through the construction of teaching plans.
  • Consulting mechanism: Establish a consultant group with outstanding department friends to guide the direction of the field.
The above is the vision of the department
