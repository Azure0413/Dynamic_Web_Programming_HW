Profile(Future Outlook)

The Department of Transportation Management Science of National Cheng Kung University was established in the 44th year of the Republic of China. It is the first transportation professional study program in Taiwan. The educational mission of this department is based on the school motto of National Cheng Kung University, "Practice knowledge through reason", the school's educational goals and the educational mission of the School of Management. The foundation is established by considering global human resources and market demand.

According to the school's educational goals, the School of Management formulates its educational mission from a global perspective. From a global perspective, it teaches students to look at the world, connect with the international standards, and promote the balanced development of industry and research. In the era of knowledge economy, science and technology are developing rapidly, and science and technology are combined with transportation and telecommunications industries to continuously create new services.

The Traffic Management Department/Electrical Management Department inherits the mission of the School of Management and is committed to cultivating students with international outlook, professional ability, service enthusiasm, and high moral character. With the spirit of inheriting the experience and inheritance of the traffic management department, we can absorb new knowledge and surpass ourselves.

Therefore, the educational vision and mission of the department are as follows：

educational vision

  • surpass oneself
  • Create the future
  • Action implementation

educational goals

  • Adhering to the tradition of transportation justice and inheriting the past and opening up the future, in the globalized economic environment, cultivate transportation and communication professionals with global outlook and innovative ability.
  • The Bachelor of Traffic Management program aims to provide a high-quality education in transport and communication management and a foundation for life-long learning.
  • The master's program of the Department of Traffic Management aims to train students to become managers of regional, national and international transportation organizations. The course emphasizes teamwork, innovative problem-solving skills, the cultivation of a global outlook, and proficiency in information technology.
  • The doctoral program of the Department of Transportation Management aims to cultivate high-quality research talents in the professional fields of transportation and communication management in academia, industry and government departments.
